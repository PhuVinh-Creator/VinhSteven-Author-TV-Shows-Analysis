# TV-Shows-Analysis
TV Shows Performance Evaluation - Power Bi dashboards

## Overview 📋

This project presents an interactive business intelligence dashboard analyzing **global TV show trends**, covering popularity, genre distribution, production history, and creator performance. The dashboard is designed with **Power BI bookmarks** for a cleaner, more structured presentation experience — allowing users to seamlessly switch between filtered views (All, 90's, 2000's) without cluttering the report canvas.

---

## Dashboard Features 🖥️

**KPI Cards:**
- **Average Popularity:** 6.77
- **Most Productive Creator:** 2SpotStudio
- **Most Common Genre:** Action & Adventure
- **Most Popular Genre:** Action & Adventure
- **Most Popular TV Show:** The Late Show with Stephen Colbert

**Visuals Included:**
- Count of Adult Shows (donut chart)
- Average Popularity by Dates (line chart, 1900–2046)
- Shows' Total Popularity by Genre (horizontal bar chart)
- Count of Shows by Genre (horizontal bar chart)
- TOP 10 Shows by Popularity (horizontal bar chart)
- Count of New TV Shows by Dates (area/line chart, 1920–2046)

---

## Filters & Interactivity 🎛️

- **Filter by Air Year** — range slider (1900–2046)
- **Filter by TV Show** — dropdown to select a specific show
- **Filter by Production Country** — dropdown for country-level filtering
- **Era Bookmarks** — quick-switch buttons for **All**, **90's**, and **2000's** views
- **Drill-Through** — select a genre in any chart to drill through for deeper genre-level insights

---

## Bookmarks 🔖

This dashboard uses **Power BI bookmarks** to enhance the presentation flow. Instead of requiring manual filter adjustments, bookmarks allow one-click navigation between pre-configured views:

- **All** — displays the full dataset across all time periods
- **90's** — filters the dashboard to shows airing in the 1990s
- **2000's** — filters the dashboard to shows airing in the 2000s

This approach keeps the report clean and makes it more intuitive for non-technical audiences to explore the data.

---

## Tools Used 🛠️

- **Power BI** — dashboard design, bookmarks, drill-through, and interactive visualizations
- **TV Shows Dataset** — includes show names, genres, air dates, popularity scores, production countries, and creator information

---

## Key Insights 🔍

- **Drama** leads all genres in both total popularity and show count, followed closely by Comedy
- **The Late Show with Stephen Colbert** is the most popular TV show in the dataset with a popularity score near 4K
- New TV show production saw a dramatic surge around **2020**, followed by a sharp decline — likely reflecting pandemic-era production disruption
- **Action & Adventure** is both the most common and most popular genre overall
- **2SpotStudio** stands out as the most productive creator in the dataset
- Average popularity spiked notably around **1960** and again in the **2000s**, reflecting major eras of television growth
